Volleyball Tournament of Student

A volleyball tournament between high school student in Bontang, east borneo at kampung baru volleyball yard. The tournament is held for 6 days starting on January 15, 2020 and ending on January 21, 2020. The opening ceremony was led by the mayor of bontang city. Tournamen are followed by sereval high school in Bontang. All high school want to be a winner, everyone do their best to get the trophy. Before the tournament, everyone had been exercising and through various other tournament to prepare for this prestigious tournament in Bontang. One by one eliminated and finally the final match between SMA 2 (my school team) and SMK 1 on January 21, 2020. The teams on final match are the best team beacuse they did their best to finaly match. The match was very fierce, each team carries out attacks and defends in order to get a score and not let the opposite team get a score. every spike, ever block, every defend performed by each team in order to become a champion. The match ends with SMA 2 as the winner with score 3-1.
